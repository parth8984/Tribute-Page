Biography

Steve Jobs is the authorized self-titled biography of American business magnate and Apple co-founder Steve Jobs. The book was written at the request of Jobs by Walter Isaacson, a former executive at CNN and TIME who had previously written best-selling biographies of Benjamin Franklin and Albert Einstein.[1][2] Based on more than forty interviews with Jobs conducted over two years—in addition to interved that he had striven to take a balanced view of his subject that did not sugarcoat Jobs's flaws.
